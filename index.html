WELCOME TO INFERENTIA

Inferentia is an artificial intelligence software house that develops, delivers and implements new technologies at the very forefront of machine learning. Our world-class team specialises in dealing with the challenge of machine-based, intelligent decision making when data is scarce or complex. Inferentia was founded in 2015 by internationally-renowned machine learning scientists Neil Lawrence, Zhenwen Dai, Andreas Damianou and Javier González.
